About us

In January 2015, we opened our doors and our hearts to the community and everyone gave their love back. Being a fine dining restaurant, Goldberg's Grill has something for everyone. It is an upscale place, a meeting place, a place where you can come and experience a unique Culinary experience right here in Lakewood.

The family behind Goldberg's Grill have been innovators and pioneers in the hospitality industry for over 15 years. With the opening of Goldberg's Grill, we are committed to enhancing our patrons' experience by providing a Fine Dining Experience. Our extensive menu made with local, fresh ingredients and our world class desserts will leave your palate yearning for more.
